14 Phere

Woahh...Yet another masterpiece from Devanshu Singh .

As always Vikrant Massey again steals the stage single handedly but this time it was tough to say he alone left the audience blank to say something for their superb act.

Kriti Kharbanda as usual made her act over dramatic at the end of the story but was not so noticeable cause she acted decently throughtout the movie .

The supporting lead also worked fluently ; Gauahar Khan & Jameel Khan were perfect fit for their roles , apart from them each and every character showed maturity and sensibility with their roles even it is a 30 second role .

The casting was picture perfect and the talent of actors itself made the movie beautiful .

Script was above average and the story was somewhere unnecessarily dragged but it was Okay as the story completed within 2 hrs .

The only thing I didn't liked was they completed their College Romance with a single song "Hum Dono Yun Mile" , i believe if the college romance could have given us something more about them then it woulb be great but nevertheless they very efficiently showed their college life through 3-5 minute song .

I would definitely ask you to binge watch 14 Phere over Hungama 2
